# Transfer Pricing Rules

## Objective
Ensure all related-party transactions are conducted at arm's length.

## Safe Ranges
- Management fees: 5% - 15%
- Product supply markup: 3% - 8%
- Logistics markup: 5% - 12%
- IP royalty: 1% - 3%

## Guardrails
- One agreement per relationship
- Rates fixed for the financial year
- Full documentation retained for 6 years
