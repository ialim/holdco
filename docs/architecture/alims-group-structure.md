# Alims Group Structure

## Group Overview
**Alims Group Limited (HoldCo)** is the parent company and strategic controller of the Alims Group.
It owns 100% of the following subsidiaries, each with a clearly defined operational and tax function.

- Alims Perfume Trading Limited
- Alims Retail Stores Limited
- Alims Reseller Network Limited
- Alims Digital Commerce Limited
- Alims Logistics & Distribution Limited


Each company maintains **independent books**, bank accounts, and statutory tax filings.
Intercompany transactions are governed strictly by approved agreements.

---

## Subsidiary Functions (Authoritative)

### 1. Alims Group Limited (Holding Company)
**Role:** Strategy, control, IP, and profit optimization

**Core Functions**
- Group strategy and long-term planning
- Ownership of brand, trademarks, and intellectual property
- Ownership of software systems and business processes
- Group accounting policies and consolidation
- Tax planning, transfer pricing governance, and compliance oversight
- Board governance and risk management

**Does NOT**
- Trade inventory
- Sell to customers
- Hold operational staff at scale

**Earns Revenue From**
- Management service fees
- Brand / IP licensing (if activated)

---

### 2. Alims Perfume Trading Limited (Central Procurement & Trading)
**Role:** Central sourcing and inventory origin point

**Core Functions**
- Local and international procurement of perfumes and related products
- Supplier negotiations and credit management
- Importation, clearing, and FX exposure management
- Central warehouse intake and quality control
- Cost normalization before internal distribution

**Risk Profile**
- FX risk
- Supplier dependency
- Inventory holding risk (pre-distribution)

**Earns Revenue From**
- Cost-plus markup on internal product supply to other subsidiaries

---

### 3. Alims Retail Stores Limited (Physical B2C Retail)
**Role:** Direct-to-consumer physical retail

**Core Functions**
- Operation of physical retail outlets
- Walk-in customer sales and POS transactions
- Cash handling and store-level inventory
- Retail staff management
- In-store promotions and customer service

**Risk Profile**
- Theft and shrinkage
- High operating expenses (rent, utilities, staff)
- VAT exposure

**Earns Revenue From**
- Retail sales margin

---

### 4. Alims Reseller Network Limited (Wholesale & B2B)
**Role:** Bulk sales, reseller management, and credit distribution

**Core Functions**
- Reseller onboarding and tier management
- Wholesale pricing and quotation negotiation
- Credit limit assignment and collections
- Bulk order fulfillment
- Sales representatives and billers

**Risk Profile**
- Credit and bad-debt risk
- Margin pressure
- Inventory holding risk

**Earns Revenue From**
- Wholesale margins on bulk and reseller sales

---

### 5. Alims Digital Commerce Limited (Online & Platform Sales)
**Role:** Digital sales channels and platform monetization

**Core Functions**
- Operation of website and mobile commerce platforms
- Online order processing and payment gateway integration
- Digital marketing funnels and CRM
- Customer data management and analytics
- Platform fee monetization (future)

**Risk Profile**
- Payment disputes and chargebacks
- Platform uptime and security risk

**Earns Revenue From**
- Online sales margin
- Platform or service fees

---

### 6. Alims Logistics & Distribution Limited (Internal & External Logistics)
**Role:** Group-wide logistics and delivery execution

**Core Functions**
- Inter-company stock transfers
- Store replenishment logistics
- Last-mile delivery to customers and resellers
- Fleet ownership, dispatch, and routing
- Third-party logistics coordination

**Risk Profile**
- Fuel price volatility
- Vehicle maintenance and asset risk
- In-transit loss risk

**Earns Revenue From**
- Cost-plus logistics service fees charged to group companies

---

## Core Group Principles
- Profit is earned where risk is borne
- No subsidiary performs another's role
- All intercompany activity must be invoiced monthly
- No retroactive adjustments
- All structures must remain FIRS- and audit-defensible
